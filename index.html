THE SPOTKNACK RECIPE

Transforming how students learn and how companies hire

We strive to be different from other training programs, by standing to support students and help them to achieve what they want to be

Spotknack Talent Community which is for the students and by the Students, was initiated to give community-based learning for all the spotknack mentees through a student leadership team.

Special care is taken while preparing the mentoring roadmap to make sure self-learning and self-exploration skills get promoted.

More than career mentoring for students, who trust us, we also have a Happiness Mentor, where the founding team will be part of each batch and make sure students use the program effectively

Career mentoring programs for students through their mentors, so that they won’t be denied opportunities based on their grades. Effectively acting as a bridge between opportunities and students.
